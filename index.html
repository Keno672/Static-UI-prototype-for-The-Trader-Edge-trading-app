Trading strategy

How we read the market

The Trader Edge is built around structured market reading. We use scan data to measure bias, charts to define support and resistance zones, and macro/news to explain risk-on or risk-off conditions. The goal is not to trade everything. The goal is to filter the best daily setups and turn them into simple, high-clarity cards.
